# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 20, 2026**

Fish Care Planner ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

Fish Care Planner is a local-first application. All fish data, care plans, feeding schedules, and preferences are stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is synced through your personal Apple iCloud account. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

## 2. Notifications & Alarms

If you grant permission, Fish Care Planner uses local notifications and alarms to remind you of feeding schedules and care tasks. These features are entirely on-device.

## 3. Analytics

We may collect anonymized, non-personally-identifiable usage analytics to improve app performance. No personal data or fish information is included.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Fish Care Planner does not share any data with third-party services, advertisers, or data brokers.

## 5. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. Deleting the app removes all stored data. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Fish Care Planner does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed as a general-purpose pet care tool.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fishcareplanner.app.
